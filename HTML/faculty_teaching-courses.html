Teaching a UCORE Course

A well taught UCORE course will cause the students who successfully complete it to make noticeable progress toward realizing the WSU learning goals and outcomes.

If you are teaching a UCORE course, please use or adjust the syllabus approved by the UCORE committee as you plan your course (or contact a colleague who previously taught the course for a copy of that approved syllabus if you do not have it). Note: before adjusting the syllabus, please review the current Syllabus Checklist for requirements, recommendations, and additional resources related to course planning and syllabi.

In addition, please review:

  • UCORE handbook: consult the UCORE handbook for detailed information on teaching UCORE courses, including specifics about requirements for the particular course category (e.g., HUM, QUAN, CAPS, etc.) and for requirements for all UCORE courses.
  • Assessment expectations: make sure you are familiar with your assessment responsibilities as an instructor of a UCORE course.
  • Additional requirements for CAPS courses: consult the UCORE handbook for details. In addition, resources for designing capstone assignments are available from the Office of Assessment of Teaching and Learning.
